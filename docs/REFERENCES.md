# 参考文献

1. **Legrand, M., et al. (2012).** "Numerical and Experimental Investigation of Rotor-to-Stator Rub Interaction." *Proceedings of ASME Turbo Expo.* — RMBOPCL 模型的参考基准。

2. **Craig, R.R. and Bampton, M.C.C. (1968).** "Coupling of Substructures for Dynamic Analyses." *AIAA Journal*, 6(7), 1313–1319. — Craig-Bampton 组件模态综合法。

3. **Simo, J.C. and Hughes, T.J.R. (1998).** *Computational Inelasticity.* Springer. — 返回映射算法理论基础。

4. **Belytschko, T., Liu, W.K., and Moran, B. (2000).** *Nonlinear Finite Elements for Continua and Structures.* Wiley. — 显式中心差分时间积分。
